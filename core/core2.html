Emotional Intelligence

Long term goal

I want to be able to work remotely within a stably evolving industry with clear pathways.

What human skills would you like to see developed in yourself while at EDA?

I would like to develop my ability to work cooperatively with others. I would also like to develop my ability to work to deadlines and time boxes.

Strengths and Limitations

Independence

Strength: I work well with autonomy, and do not need to have my hand held in order to meet a challenge.

Limitation: I tend to ignore outside influences and opinions.

Analysis

Strength: I question everything and take the time to logically consider ideas before accepting them.

Limitation: I sometimes question things too much and end up being too self-critical.

Creativity

Strength: I find unconventional solutions to complicated problems.

Limitation: Sometimes I seek unnecessary solutions and these solutions can be less effective than the original way of doing things.

Reflection

Strength: I am reserved and process things internally, which allows me to communicate with precision.

Limitation: Sometimes I forget to communicate the things I process and just assume everyone is on the same page as me.

Commitment to how you will manage your workload

I will be making sure to complete all of the work within the allotted time by making sure I complete at least 9 hours of work every weekday. I will also make sure to complete at least 1 hour of study every weekend.

I will also follow through with the lessons before jumping onto the technical assignments so I can better structure how I will go about executing them. I will make sure to complete the reflections as I go, so I can communicate without fail. Finally, I will make sure to request feedback from my peers.

Commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

By using Slack to communicate regularly, I will be able to effectively communicate with my peers, facilitators and industry/community representatives.

I will make sure to check in with my facilitator every week at the scheduled time. If I have any questions that need to be answered in order to proceed with my study, I will first ask the foundations slack channel, and then talk to my facilitator.

Description of what I expect from the facilitation team

I expect reasonable support in the form of timely answered questions and direction where necessary.
